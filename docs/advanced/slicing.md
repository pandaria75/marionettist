# Slicing

Slicing is how the harness turns larger work into smaller approved units.

## A good slice should define

- goal
- allowed scope
- forbidden scope
- validation expectation
- done criteria

## Why slicing matters

- it reduces review surface area
- it keeps validation targeted
- it lowers the risk of accidental cross-cutting edits
- it makes gate decisions clearer
